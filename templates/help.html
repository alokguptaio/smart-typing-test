How is WPM calculated in typing test? ▼
Net WPM = (Correct Words + Half Mistakes×0.5) ÷ Time (minutes)

Qualify ke liye: English — 30 WPM, Hindi — 25 WPM minimum chahiye.
Full Mistake aur Half Mistake mein kya fark hai? ▼
Full Mistake: Word bilkul galat — 1 mistake count hoti hai.
Half Mistake: Word thoda galat (1-2 character difference) — 0.5 mistake count hoti hai.
